The Setlist (Curriculum)

Week 1: R... Baby One More Time

My loneliness is killing me... without Vectors. Introduction to R syntax, variables, and basic data types. We start strong.

Week 2: Oops!... I Did It Again (Debugging)

I played with your heart, got lost in the code. Handling errors and exceptions. Learning why your loops aren't looping and fixing them.

Week 3: Toxic Data Frames

With a taste of your poison, I'm on a ride. Cleaning dirty data using Tidyverse. Don't let your data be toxic; purify it.

Week 4: Gimme More (Algorithms)

Gimme, gimme more sorting. Implementing Bubble Sort and Binary Search in R. We need performance, and we need it now.
