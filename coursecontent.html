DSA in Rust

Master the art of solving problems you will never actually face in real web development.

⚠️ Warning: This course contains recursion. Side effects may include headache, confusion, and crying in the shower.

Curriculum (The Grind)

Week 1: Big O Notation

Learning how to explain why your code is slow, mathematically. We will discuss why O(n^2) makes interviewers look at you with disappointment.

Week 2: Arrays & Strings

We pretend JavaScript arrays are actual arrays (they aren't) and solve LeetCode "Easy" questions that take 4 hours.

Week 3: Linked Lists

A data structure that is useless in Rust, but we learn it because "Computer Science."

Week 4: Trees & Graphs

Inverting a Binary Tree: The only skill that matters to Google. We will also traverse graphs until you lose your mind.

Week 5: Dynamic Programming

The part where we memorize solutions because nobody actually understands this.
