DSA in Rust

Master the art of solving problems you will never actually face in real web development.

⚠️ Warning: This course contains recursion. Side effects may include headache, confusion, and crying in the shower.

Curriculum (The Grind)

Week 1: Basics

Learning how to explain why your code is slow, mathematically. We will discuss why O(n^2) makes interviewers look at you with disappointment.

Week 2: Arrays & Strings

We pretend Rust arrays are actual arrays (they aren't) and solve LeetCode "Easy" questions
